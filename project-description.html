Donation Cat

I worked on this app for my 8 day final project at Dev Bootcamp. The app is designed to gather disparate charities together in one location to enable users to more easily donate to charities. It imports charity Amazon wishlists in real time, and offers full user & charity login/logout/profile capability. The site was built with Javascript, jQuery, Ruby, Rails, AJAX, Semantic, HTML5, & CSS3.
